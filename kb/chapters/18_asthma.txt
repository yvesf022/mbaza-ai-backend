IZINA RY’INDWARA
Asthma (Umwamuka)

IBISOBANURO MU MAGAMBO MAGUFI
Asthma ni indwara yo mu myanya y’erinera (bronchi) irangwa no gukomereka no kwiyongera k’umusokoro, bigatera guhumeka nabi no gucika intege. Igaruka (recurrent) kandi ishobora gukomera mu bihe bimwe (exacerbations).

IBIMENYETSO BY’INGENZI
- Guhumeka bigoranye, cyane cyane nijoro cyangwa mugitondo
- Inkorora yongera igabanuke, rimwe na rimwe ifite umwuka w’umusokoro
- Gukomangirwa mu gituza, gucika intege vuba
- Wheeze (ijwi ryo kuziriza mu bihaha)

IBYO KWITONDERA (RED FLAGS — HAMAGARA UBUFASHA BW’IHUTIRWA)
- Guhumeka guke cyane n’ijwi rito, amaguru cyangwa iminwa biba ubururu
- Saturation (SpO2) < 90%, cyangwa Peak Flow < 33% y’icyitegererezo
- Kudashobora kuvuga interuro ndende
- Kutakenera ibikoresho byo mu rugo (inhaler) keretse ubufasha bwihuse bubonetse

IVURWA RY’IBANZE MU RUGO
- Ifunguro ririmo amazi menshi, kwirinda umwuka uhumanya n’itabi
- Gukoresha salbutamol inhaler (2–4 puffs, gusubiramo mu minota 20 niba bikenewe)
- Kumenya ibitera (allergens, umunuko, imyitozo ikabije) no kubyirinda

IVURWA RY’UMWUGA (MU KIGO N’UBUVUZI)
- Salbutamol nebulization cyangwa metered-dose inhaler + spacer
- Imiti igabanya urwuma (prednisolone PO cyangwa hydrocortisone IV, bitewe n’uburemere)
- Imiti irinda ibihe (inhaled corticosteroids, +/- LABA)
- Oxygene niba SpO2 iri hasi, kugenzura Peak Flow
- Kwiga ku buryo bwo gukoresha inhaler neza

KWIYIRINDA
- Kwirinda itabi n’umwotsi, gucunga allergens (umukungugu, ivumbi, imiti isharira)
- Gukoresha imiti yo kurinda uko yanditswe, kugenzura buri kwezi/igihe

IMYITWARIRE Y’UMUJYANAMA W’UBUZIMA
- Baza ibihe byiyongera, uko akoresha inhaler, mufashe kubika “asthma action plan”
- Muremeshe ko ashobora kubaho neza iyo akurikiranye neza imiti

INKOMOKO Y’AMAKURU
UCG Rwanda 2023, Chapter 18 (Respiratory) — ihuriro risanzwe mu buvuzi bw’ibanze.
