IZINA RY’INDWARA: Asthma (Guhumeka bigoranye bitewe n’imiyoyo y’imiyungirizo y’uruhu rwo mu bihaha)

IBISOBANURO:
Asthma iterwa n’uko imiyungirizo y’uruhu rwo mu bihaha yifunga igakazwa, igatera wheezing (gukoma nka filimbi), inkorora, no kubura umwuka.

IBIMENYETSO BY’INGENZI:
- Guhumeka bigoranye, gucika intege vuba
- Inkorora y’umugoroba cyangwa mu gitondo
- Ijwi rimeze nka filimbi mu bihaha (wheezing)

IBYO GUKORA NONAHA:
- Gukoresha inhaler ya salbutamol (blue) uko muganga yabyanditse
- Kwicara neza, gufungura imyenda igufashe
- Kwihutira kure y’imyuka yangiza (itabi, ivumbi)

RYARI KWIHUTIRA KWA MUGANGA:
- Nta mpinduka nyuma yo gukoresha inhaler
- Kubura umwuka bikabije, kuvuga bigoye
- Umwana utembera amabere ahumeka

ICYITONDERWA:
Jya ugira “asthma action plan” wandikiwe na muganga, kandi wirinde triggers.
