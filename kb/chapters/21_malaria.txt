IZINA RY’INDWARA
Malaria

IBISOBANURO
Malaria iterwa na Plasmodium, ikwirakwizwa n’umubu w’ingore (Anopheles). Igaragarira mu muriro ugaruka, umunaniro, kuribwa umutwe, no kuribwa mu ngingo.

IBIMENYETSO
- Umuriro ugaruka, kubira ibyuya, kuribwa umutwe
- Kunanuka, isesemi, kuribwa mu ngingo
- Mu bana: kugaragaza umunaniro mwinshi, kutanywa

RED FLAGS
- Severe malaria: coma, convulsions, hypoglycemia, anemia ikabije, icterus
- Umuriro udacogora nubwo imiti yatangiye

IVURWA RY’IBANZE
- Kwipimisha (RDT cyangwa smear) mbere yo gutanga imiti
- Kugumisha amazi, paracetamol ku muriro

IVURWA RY’UMWUGA
- ACT (Artemisinin-based Combination Therapy) ku byoroheje
- Artesunate IV ku byikabije, monitoring y’amasukari n’amaraso
- Gukosora anemia, kugenzura ingaruka

KWIYIRINDA
- Imitego y’uduseke turinda imibu (LLINs), gusukura aho imibu ikurira
- Repellents, kwirinda gusohoka nimugoroba na mu gicuku

UMUJYANAMA W’UBUZIMA
- Sobanurira igipimo, uko imiti ikoreshwa, igihe cyo kugaruka niba bidahindutse
