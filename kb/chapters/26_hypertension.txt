IZINA: Umuvuduko w'amaraso (Hypertension)

IBISOBANURO (mu magambo make):
— Ni ukwiyongera kudasanzwe k'umuvuduko w’amaraso ≥140/90 mmHg (ku bantu bakuru) ku bipimo by’amasaha atandukanye.

IBIMENYETSO BY’INGENZI:
— Akenshi ntibigaragara. Hari igihe habaho umutwe, kuruka, kureba ibihu, gucika intege, kubyimba imirundi (edema).

IBYIHUTIRWA (“RED FLAGS”):
— Umuvuduko mwinshi cyane >180/120 mmHg ufite: kuribwa umutima, guhumeka nabi, kuvunagurika mu maso/ikiganza/cy’akaguru, kuvuga nabi, umutwe ukabije, cyangwa impiswi z’amaraso. Yoherezwa byihuse (emergency).

IBIPIMO BY’INGENZI:
— BP yipimwa neza (gupima inshuro nyinshi), BMI, isukari mu maraso, lipids, umusaruro w’impyiko (creatinine, eGFR), urea, electrolytes, EKG niba bibaye ngombwa, proteinuriya muri urine.

UBURYO BWO KUBUNGABUNGA/IVURWA RUSANGE:
— Guhindura imibereho: kugabanya umunyu, kurya indyo iboneye (imboga, imbuto), kugabanya ibisindisha, kureka itabi, imyitozo 150min/icyumweru, kugabanya ibiro.
— IMITI: thiazide (hydrochlorothiazide), ACE-inhibitors (eg. enalapril), ARB (losartan), calcium-channel blocker (amlodipine), cyangwa kombinasi bitewe n’urwego.
— Intego ya BP: <140/90 ku benshi, <130/80 ku bafite diyabete/indwara z’impyiko (nk’uko muganga yabisuzuma).

ICYO KWITONDERA:
— Kugenzura imiti n’ingaruka (kubura potassium, inkovu y’uruhu, n’ibindi), gusuzuma iyo utwite, n’abana bisaba uburyo butandukanye.
— Ibisubizo bitangwa hano ni rusange; ganiriza muganga wawe wemewe.
