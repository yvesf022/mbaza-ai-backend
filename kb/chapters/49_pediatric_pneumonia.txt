IZINA RY’INDWARA: Pneumonia mu bana

1) IBISOBANURO
Ukwandura mu bihaha butera guhumeka nabi mu bana (akenshi bacterial/viral).

2) IBIMENYETSO
Inkoni z’umwuka, umwuka wihuta, guhumeka nabi, fevere, inzozi nkeya, cyanosis mu byago bikomeye.

3) ISUZUMA
• IMCI criteria: fast breathing, chest indrawing, danger signs.
• O2 saturation, X-ray niba bibashoboka.

4) KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
• Amoxicillin 40–50 mg/kg/dose PO bid 5–7 iminsi ku kidakomeye; azithromycin/alternatives bitewe n’aho mutuye.
• O2 nibiba bikenewe, fluids, ibinini bya fevere.
• Kohereza niba ari “severe” cyangwa hari danger signs.

5) IGIHE USHAKA UMWUGA
Guhumeka nabi cyane, cyanosis, kutanywa, convulsions, sepsis, O2 < 90%.

6) KWIRINDA
Inkingo (PCV, Hib, measles), imirire myiza, isuku, kwirinda umwotsi/itabi.

7) IBYITONDERWA
Kwitondera doses zishingiye ku biro; kureba imikoranire y’imiti.
