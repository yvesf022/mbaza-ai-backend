IZINA RY’INDWARA
Peptic Ulcer Disease (PUD)

IBISOBANURO
PUD ni ibisebe mu gifu/duodenum akenshi bitewe na H. pylori cyangwa ibinini bya NSAIDs. Bitera kuribwa mu nda hejuru, bikiyongera iyo ushonye cyangwa nyuma yo kurya ibiryo bimwe.

IBIMENYETSO
- Kuribwa mu nda (epigastric pain), isesemi, kwiyongera nyuma yo kurya cyangwa mu gicuku
- Rimwe na rimwe: kuzana amaraso, umwijima mu musarane

RED FLAGS
- Hematemesis, melena, gucika intege, hypotension
- Kuribwa gukomeye kudatuza, gukuka kw’igifu (perforation) bikekwa

IVURWA RY’IBANZE
- Kwihutira kwipimisha H. pylori (stool antigen/urea breath test)
- Kwimakaza PPIs, kwirinda NSAIDs, itabi, ibisindisha

IVURWA RY’UMWUGA
- Eradication therapy ya H. pylori (triple/quadruple) nk’uko UCG ibisaba
- Endoscopy ku red flags cyangwa kudakira

KWIYIRINDA
- Kwirinda ibinini byangiza igifu utabivuganye na muganga
- Indyo iboneye, kugabanya ibisindisha

UMUJYANAMA W’UBUZIMA
- Gahunda yo kunywa imiti neza, igihe cyo kugaruka mu ivuriro

INKOMOKO
UCG Rwanda 2023, Chapter 23.
