IZINA: Impiswi z’abana n’inyota (Pediatric Diarrhea & Dehydration)

IBISOBANURO:
— Impiswi nyinshi zitera kubura amazi, zishobora guterwa na virus (rotavirus), bacteria, parasites.

IBIMENYETSO:
— Impiswi, kuruka, umunaniro, umunwa wumye, inkari nke, isura yijimye; ku mubyeyi: agasanduku k’inkweto kameze ubusa (diaper dry).

RED FLAGS:
— Ibimenyetso by’inyota ikabije: kubura ubwenge, kutabona umutima neza, impiswi z’amaraso, umuriro mwinshi.

IBIPIMO:
— Gusuzuma urwego rw’inyota (mild/moderate/severe), labor niba bikenewe.

IVURWA:
— ORS (oral rehydration solution) ku manywa kenshi; zinc 10–14days; indyo ikomeza (kugaburira gake gake). IV fluids ku nyota ikabije.

ICYO KWITONDERA:
— Kwirinda: isuku y’amazi, gukaraba intoki, inkingo (rotavirus).
