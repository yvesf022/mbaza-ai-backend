IZINA RY’IGITABO: 41. EPILEPSY

A. IBISOBANURO
Epilepsy ni indwara y’ihungabana ry’imitsi riterwa n’imirabyo y’amashanyarazi mu bwonko, itera ibitero (seizures) byongera.

B. IBIMENYETSO
- Generalized/partial seizures, post-ictal confusion, kugwa, kumira ururimi.

C. ISUZUMA
- Anamnesis (ubwo byatangiye, imyaka, ubwitabire), glucose, electrolytes; EEG/CT/MRI niba bibonetse.

D. KUVURA
- First line: valproate, carbamazepine, lamotrigine bitewe n’ubwoko; kubahiriza dose. 
- Status epilepticus: diazepam/lorazepam IV/rectal; kohereza.

E. USHAKA UMWUGA
- Seizure irambye >5 min, kumurika kenshi, trauma, ku bana bato cyane.

F. KWIRINDA
- Kwirinda gusinzira gake, inzoga nyinshi, ibintu byatera ibitero (umucyo uhishe).

G. IBYITONDERWA
- Gutozwa ibyangombwa byo gutabara mu rugo no kwirinda impanuka mu gihe cy’igitero.
