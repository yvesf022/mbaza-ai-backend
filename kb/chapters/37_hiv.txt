IZINA RY’IGITABO: 37. VIH/SIDA

A. IBISOBANURO
VIH ni virusi igabanya ubudahangarwa, ishobora kugera ku cyiciro cya SIDA mu gihe itavuwe neza.

B. IBIMENYETSO BY’INGENZI
- Gukama kw’imbaraga, indwara zagaruka kenshi, indwara zo mu kanwa (oral thrush), ibibyimba.

C. ISUZUMA
- Rapid tests/ELISA, CD4/VL niba bibonetse. 
- Screening zindi: HBV, HCV, syphilis, TB.

D. KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
- ART hakurikijwe amabwiriza y’igihugu (TDF/3TC/DTG n’ibindi). 
- Cotrimoxazole prophylaxis aho bikenewe, inkingo zose.

E. USHAKA UMWUGA
- Opportunistic infections zikomeye, kutihanganira imiti, igabanuka rikabije ry’imbaraga.

F. KWIRINDA
- Kondomu, kuboneza urubyaro, kudasangira ibikoresho byandura, gusuzuma igihe.

G. IBYITONDERWA
- Guhabwa inama ku burenganzira n’ubuzima bw’imyitwarire; kurindwa ivangura.
