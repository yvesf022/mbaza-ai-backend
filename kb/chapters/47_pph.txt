IZINA RY’INDWARA: Postpartum Hemorrhage (PPH) — kuva kwinshi nyuma yo kubyara

1) IBISOBANURO
Kuva >500 ml (vaginal) cyangwa >1000 ml (CS) mu masaha 24 ya mbere.

2) IBIMENYETSO
Kuva kugaragara, hypotension, tachycardia, pallor, gucika intege.

3) ISUZUMA
• Reba impamvu 4 Ts: Tone (uterine atony), Tissue (placenta isigaye), Trauma (imyanya y’igitsina), Thrombin (kwirinda amaraso kudakora).

4) KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
• Uterine massage, oxytocin 10 IU IM/IV, misoprostol 800–1000 µg PR, tranexamic acid 1 g IV vuba.
• Gushaka aho amaraso ava, guhagarika ibikomere, fluids IV.

5) IGIHE USHAKA UMWUGA
Iyo kuva gukomeje/kwiyongera, shock, cyangwa bikenewe ibindi bikoresho.

6) KWIRINDA
AMTSL: oxytocin nyuma yo kubyara, traction yoroheje ya umbilical cord, kugenzura placenta yuzuye.

7) IBYITONDERWA
Kuba hafi y’imiti n’amazi, kugenzura vital signs kenshi.
