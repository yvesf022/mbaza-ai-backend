IZINA RY’INDWARA: Uburwayi bw’umutima bugenza nabi amaraso (Heart Failure)

1) IBISOBANURO
Umutima utakigira imbaraga zo gusunika amaraso neza (systolic) cyangwa warambutse (diastolic).

2) IBIMENYETSO
Kuribwa n’Igituza giterwa n’umunaniro, guhumeka nabi by’umwihariko nijoro, kubyimba ibirenge, kunanuka vuba, kuryama agahinda ko kubura umwuka (orthopnea).

3) ISUZUMA
• Vital signs, BMI, kugenzura shuga na BP.
• BNP/NT-proBNP niba bibashoboka.
• ECG, X-ray y’Igituza: cardiomegaly/pulmonary edema.
• Echocardiography (niba ibonetse) kugira ngo urebe EF.

4) KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
• Furosemide 20–40 mg ( kugeza hejuru bitewe n’amazi).
• ACEI (enalapril) cyangwa ARB (losartan) + beta-blocker (carvedilol cyangwa bisoprolol) iyo ashobora kwihangana.
• Aldosterone antagonist (spironolactone) ku bafite EF iri hasi.
• Inama z’imibereho: kugabanya umunyu, kugenzura amazi, kugabanya ibisindisha, siporo yoroheje.

5) IGIHE USHAKA UMWUGA
Guhumeka nabi bikabije, cyanosis, edema ikabije, hypotension, arythmie ikomeye, cyangwa kutihanganira imiti.

6) KWIRINDA
Kugenzura BP, shuga, kureka itabi, gufata imiti neza, kugenzura ibiro.

7) IBYITONDERWA
Witondere amafasha (NSAIDs), gukora monitoring ya kreatinine na K+ ku bafata ACEI/ARB/spironolactone.
