IZINA RY’INDWARA: Stroke (Kuvuga cyangwa Kwimuka bigoye bitewe n’amaraso mu bwonko)

1) IBISOBANURO
Kwuzura cyangwa kubura amaraso mu bwonko (ischemic cyangwa hemorrhagic).

2) IBIMENYETSO
FAST: Face (imbabura mu isura), Arm (ikiganza/gitugu kitizerera), Speech (amagambo apfupfu), Time (vugana n’abaganga ako kanya).

3) ISUZUMA
• Vital signs (harimo glycemia yihuta).
• CT scan/MRI niba bibashoboka (gutandukanya ischemic na hemorrhagic).
• Labs: CBC, glucose, lipids, INR.

4) KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
• Oneka glycemia (wirinda hypoglycemia/hyperglycemia).
• Aspirin 150–300 mg iminsi ya mbere ku ischemic stroke niba nta hemorrhage.
• BP management ku cyiciro gitunganye (wirinda kugabanya cyane).
• Reba kunyunyuta, imirire, mobilization yoroheje, physiotherapy.

5) IGIHE USHAKA UMWUGA
Igihe cyose utekereza stroke nshyashya, guhumeka nabi, kugabanuka kw’iby’ingenzi, hemorrhage, cyangwa ibimenyetso byiyongera.

6) KWIRINDA
Genzura BP, shuga, lipids, kureka itabi, aspirin/statin uko biteganyijwe.

7) IBYITONDERWA
tPA/Intervention bikeneye ubuvuzi bwisumbuye, reba nka guidelines z’igihugu.
