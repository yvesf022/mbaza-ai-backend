IZINA: Ihungabana n’akababaro k’umutima (Depression/Anxiety)

IBISOBANURO:
— Indwara z’imitekerereze: guhangayika, gucika intege mu byishimo n’imirimo, ibitekerezo byo kwiheba, ubwoba buhoraho.

IBIMENYETSO:
— Kutagira ibyo wishimira, umunaniro, ibitotsi bihinduka, kwigunga, kutiyizera, ibitekerezo byo kwiyahura (emergency).

RED FLAGS:
— Ibitekerezo/intego byo kwiyahura cyangwa kwica undi. Ubutabazi bwihuse, kubonana n’inzobere mu buzima bwo mu mutwe.

IBIPIMO:
— PHQ-9, GAD-7 (niba biboneka), isuzuma ry’imiti n’izindi ndwara zifatanye.

IVURWA:
— Inama n’ubufasha (counseling/CBT), SSRIs/SNRIs ku babikeneye, imyitozo ngororamubiri, guhuza n’itsinda ry’inkunga.

ICYO KWITONDERA:
— Kugira umujyanama w’ubuzima, kwirinda kunywa cyane ibisindisha, kugenzura imiti.
