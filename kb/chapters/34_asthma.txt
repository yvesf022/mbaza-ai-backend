IZINA RY’IGITABO: 34. ASTHMA

A. IBISOBANURO
Asthma ni indwara yo guhumeka iterwa no kwifunga kwa bronchi ku buryo buhindagurika, itera guhekenya (wheezing), gucika umwuka no gukorora cyane, cyane nijoro cyangwa nimugoroba.

B. IBIMENYETSO BY’INGENZI
- Gucika umwuka, guhekenya, gukorora kenshi, kugaruka kw’ibimenyetso. 
- Ibyongera: umukungugu, imibu/inyungu, imbeho, imyitozo, itabi, umunuko ukaze.

C. ISUZUMA
- Anamnesis + isuzuma ry’umubiri (wheezing). 
- SpO2, Peak flow/FEV1 niba bibonetse. 
- Reba ibimenyetso by’ubukana (SpO2<92%, guhindura ibara, kuvuga amagambo make).

D. KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
- Icyihutirwa: Salbutamol neb/inhaler (puffs 4–8 q20min ×1h), O2 niba SpO2<94%.
- Igihe cyose: ICS (beclomethasone/budesonide) ku bababara kenshi, salbutamol PRN.
- Gutoza gukoresha inhaler, gutahukana “asthma action plan.”

E. USHAKA UMWUGA NIBA BIKENEWE
- Asthma y’ubukana (cyanosis, SpO2<92%, guhinduka mu mutwe, kutavuga), kutitabara nyuma y’amasaha 1 y’ivuriro ryo hasi, umwana muto cyane.

F. KWIRINDA
- Kwizirika ku mpamvu z’ihembe (dust, itabi), gukingira umwuka, gukingira ibicurane, gukoresha inhaler neza.

G. IBYITONDERWA
- Ibisubizo ni rusange; kugenzurwa na muganga ni ingenzi mu gukwirakwiza urwego rw’ivuriro.
