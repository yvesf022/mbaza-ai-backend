IZINA RY’INDWARA: Umuvuduko w’amaraso uri hejuru (Hypertension)

1) IBISOBANURO
Umuvuduko w’amaraso uri hejuru ni uko umuvuduko (BP) urenga 140/90 mmHg ku nshuro ebyiri cyangwa se 130/80 ku bafite diyabete/uburwayi bw’umutima. Utera ibindi byago: stroke, indwara z’umutima, impyiko.

2) IBIMENYETSO
Akenshi nta bimenyetso. Hari igihe: umutwe, gucika intege, guhumeka nabi iyo hejuru cyane, kubyimbirwa n’ibirenge.

3) ISUZUMA
• Fata BP neza (icupa rikwiye, umuntu yicaye 5 min, kuboko ku mutima). 
• Sura amasuzuma y’ibanze: HbA1c cyangwa fasting glucose, lipids, kreatinine/eGFR, umusemburo mu nkari (proteinurie).
• Reba ibindi byago (itabi, ibiro, imirire, siporo).

4) KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
• Guhindura imibereho: kugabanya umunyu, kurya indyo iboneye (DASH), gukora siporo nibura iminota 150/icyumweru, kugabanya ibisindisha, gutakaza 5–10% by’ibiro.
• Imiti (hitamo imwe; wongeraho bitewe n’ibisubizo):
  – Thiazide (hydrochlorothiazide 12.5–25 mg/j)
  – ACE inhibitor (enalapril 5–20 mg/j) cyangwa ARB (losartan 25–100 mg/j)
  – CCB (amlodipine 5–10 mg/j)
• Intego: <140/90 (cyangwa <130/80 ku bafite ibyago byinshi).
• Genza neza imiti n’ingaruka: amacandwe, kubyimba ku biganza/ibirenge, gukorora (ACEI).

5) IGIHE USHAKA UMWUGA
BP ≥180/120 mmHg ifite ibimenyetso (umutwe ukabije, kugoreka ijisho, guhumeka nabi), eGFR igabanutse cyane, proteinurie nyinshi, mutima urembye, inda (preeclampsia).

6) KWIRINDA
Kureka itabi, kugabanya umunyu <5 g/j, siporo, kugabanya stress, gukurikirana BP buri kwezi kugeza igengewe.

7) IBYITONDERWA
Reba imikoranire n’indi miti (NSAIDs byongera BP), wite ku bagore batwite (wirinda ACEI/ARBs).
