IZINA: Stroke (CVA)

IBISOBANURO:
— Kwangirika k’udutsi tw’amaraso mu bwonko bitewe no gufungana (ischemic) cyangwa kuruka kw’amaraso (hemorrhagic).

IBIMENYETSO BYIHUSE (FAST):
— F: isura igoramye, A: ukuboko/gutambuka bikagorwa, S: kuvuga bikagora, T: igihe ni ingenzi.
— Umutwe ukabije, kureba ibihu, guhagarika inkari/impiswi, cyangwa kubura ubwenge.

RED FLAGS:
— Ibimenyetso byavuzwe haruguru byatangiriye vuba; ukeneye serivisi z’ubutabazi ako kanya (window yo gutanga trombolysis ni nto).

IBIPIMO:
— CT-scan/ MRI y’ubwonko, glucose yihuse, EKG, laboratoire isanzwe.

IVURWA RUSANGE:
— Ischemic: antiplatelet (aspirin) niba bidahabanye n’ibisubizo bya CT; gucunga umuvuduko w’amaraso; rehabilitation.
— Hemorrhagic: gucunga umuvuduko, neuro-surgery niba bikenewe.
— Kwirinda: kugabanya umuvuduko, isukari, itabi, indyo n’imyitozo, statins ku bakeneye.

ICYO KWITONDERA:
— Kumenya ibimenyetso vuba no kwihutira ubutabazi ni ingenzi.
