IZINA: Indwara zandurira mu mibonano mpuzabitsina (STIs)

IBISOBANURO:
— Gonorrhea, chlamydia, syphilis, trichomonas, HSV, HPV, n’izindi.

IBIMENYETSO:
— Kuvuvutira/ububabare mu gitsina, kwishimagura, ibisebe, impagarara mu nda (PID).

RED FLAGS:
— Umuriro, kubabara mu nda bikabije, sepsis; ukeneye gutabara vuba.

IBIPIMO:
— Tests zishingiye ku buryo buboneka (NAATs ku GC/CT, RPR/VDRL ku syphilis, wet mount ku trichomonas, HIV test).

IVURWA:
— Gonorrhea: ceftriaxone + doxycycline ku gukingira chlamydia.
— Chlamydia: doxycycline/azithromycin.
— Syphilis: penicillin G.
— PID: kombinasi ya antibiotics. Kwigisha ku kwirinda, gukoresha agakingirizo, no kuvura abo mwaryamanye.

ICYO KWITONDERA:
— Gusubira kwisuzumisha nyuma ya 3 mois kuri bamwe, gutanga inama z’ubuzima bw’imyororokere.
