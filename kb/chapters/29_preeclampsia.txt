IZINA: Preeclampsia/Eclampsia

IBISOBANURO:
— Preeclampsia: umuvuduko ukabije ≥140/90 nyuma ya w’icyumweru 20 cy’inda + proteinuriya/ibimenyetso by’impyiko/by’uturemangingo.
— Eclampsia: preeclampsia + k seizures.

IBIMENYETSO:
— Umutwe ukabije, kureba ibihu, kubabara mu nda y’ibumoso hejuru (epigastric/RUQ), kubyimba mu maso n’intoki, ibipimo bya BP byakunze kuzamuka.

RED FLAGS:
— Seizures, BP >160/110, ibimenyetso by’iwangirika ry’impyiko/platelets nkeya. Ubutabazi bwihuse, magnesium sulfate, gucunga BP, no gutegura isohoka ry’umwana bitewe n’urwego.

IBIPIMO:
— BP z’umpuririze, urine protein, creatinine, platelet count, LFTs, ultrasound ya fetusi, non-stress test (niba biboneka).

IVURWA:
— MgSO4 ku kurinda/gukemura seizure, antihypertensives (labetalol, hydralazine), gukurikirana umwana n’umubyeyi; igihe cyo kubyarira gigenwa n’urwego rw’indwara.

ICYO KWITONDERA:
— Igenzura rihoraho mu nda, kumenya ibimenyetso byo kugaruka (postpartum preeclampsia irashoboka).
