IZINA RY’INDWARA
Low Back Pain

IBISOBANURO
Kuribwa mu nda y’inyuma (lumbosacral area) akenshi bituruka ku misokoro/imyitozo idahwitse, imyanya y’imitsi, arthritis, cyangwa disk issues. Akenshi bikira mu byumweru bike n'ubufasha bworoshye.

IBIMENYETSO
- Kuribwa mu nda y’inyuma, kwiyongera iyo wicaye/uhagaze igihe kirekire
- Rimwe na rimwe: kurengerana mu kuguru (sciatica)

RED FLAGS
- Kunanirwa kugenga ibihaha/imyanda, numbness mu gace k'ukuguru (cauda equina)
- Umuriro, history ya kanseri, trauma ikomeye

IVURWA RY’IBANZE
- Analgesics (paracetamol/NSAIDs niba nta contraindications)
- Imyitozo yo kugorora, kuryama neza, kugabanya imitsi ihahamuka
- Ubujyanama ku mikorere y'akazi n'uko wicaraga/hagaze

IVURWA RY’UMWUGA
- Physiotherapy; imaging niba red flags cyangwa kutakira
- Referral kuri specialist niba bikomeje

KWIYIRINDA
- Ergonomics, imyitozo yoroheje ihoraho, kugabanya ibiro

UMUJYANAMA W’UBUZIMA
- Remeza ko ibimenyetso byinshi bikira, ariko wite ku red flags

INKOMOKO
UCG Rwanda 2023, Chapter 25.
