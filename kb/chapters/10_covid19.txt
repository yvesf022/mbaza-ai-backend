IZINA: COVID-19

IBISOBANURO:
Indwara yandurira mu myanya y’ubuhumekero iterwa na SARS-CoV-2.

IBIMENYETSO:
- Umuriro, inkorora, guhumeka bigoye
- Kubura uburyohe/impumuro, umunaniro

IBYO GUKORA NONAHA:
- Gupima, kuruhuka, kunywa amazi, paracetamol
- Gushyira intera, kwambara agapfukamunwa uko bikwiye

RYARI KWIHUTIRA:
- Guhumeka bigoye, igitutu cyo mu bihaha kiri hasi (SpO2 < 92% niba upima)

ICYITONDERWA:
Urukingo n’isuku birinda; kumvira inama za serivisi z’ubuzima.
