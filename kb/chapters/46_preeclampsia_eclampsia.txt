IZINA RY’INDWARA: Preeclampsia/Eclampsia

1) IBISOBANURO
Preeclampsia: BP ≥140/90 nyuma ya wks 20 + proteinurie cyangwa ibimenyetso by’impyiko/umusokoro. Eclampsia: Preeclampsia + kubura ubwenge/ibise (seizures).

2) IBIMENYETSO
Umutwe ukabije, kureba ibyijimye, kuribwa epigastrique, kubyimba mu maso/ibirenge, BP iri hejuru, proteinurie.

3) ISUZUMA
• BP kenshi, dipstick protein, labs: platelets, AST/ALT, kreatinine.
• FHR monitoring, reba gahunda yo kohereza.

4) KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
• MgSO4 (loading/maintenance) ku kibazo cya eclampsia/severe preeclampsia.
• Labetalol cyangwa hydralazine kugabanya BP hejuru cyane.
• Gutegura kohereza ku rwego rwo hejuru no gutekereza ku gihe cyo kubyara.

5) IGIHE USHAKA UMWUGA
Byose by’eclampsia/severe preeclampsia bisaba kohereza ako kanya.

6) KWIRINDA
ANC kare, kumenya ibimenyetso by’ingaruka, kugenzura BP.

7) IBYITONDERWA
MgSO4 isabwa kuba ifashwe neza, kurinda aspiration, monitoring y’uruhu/PND/reflexes.
