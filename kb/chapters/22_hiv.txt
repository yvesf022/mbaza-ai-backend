IZINA RY’INDWARA
HIV/VIH

IBISOBANURO
HIV ni virusi itera SIDA, igahungabanya ubudahangarwa bw’umubiri. Ivurwa neza binyuze mu miti ya ARVs kugira ngo virusi igabanuke kandi ubuzima bugendere neza.

IBIMENYETSO
- Ibinure, umuriro ugaruka, kunanuka, infections zigaruka
- Opportunistic infections (candidiasis, TB, PCP)

RED FLAGS
- Ibimenyetso by’infekisiyo ikaze (pneumonia, meningitis)
- CD4 igabanuka cyane, viral load iri hejuru cyane

IVURWA RY’IBANZE
- Kwipimisha no kumenya uko uhagaze
- Kwandikwa vuba kuri ART nyuma yo gusuzuma (Treat All)

IVURWA RY’UMWUGA
- ART regimen hakurikijwe UCG/PMTCT/Guidelines
- Cotrimoxazole prophylaxis uko bikwiye
- Gukurikirana adherence, counseling, gusuzuma co-infections

KWIYIRINDA
- Condom, gukoresha serivisi z’ubujyanama no kwipimisha kenshi
- PMTCT ku bagore batwite, PrEP ku bafite ibyago byinshi

UMUJYANAMA W’UBUZIMA
- Remeza ko kubaho neza bishoboka ukoresheje ART, shishikariza adherence 95%+

INKOMOKO
UCG Rwanda 2023, Chapter 22.
