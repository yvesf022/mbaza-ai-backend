IZINA RY’INDWARA
Tuberculosis (Igituntu)

IBISOBANURO
TB ni infekisiyo iterwa na Mycobacterium tuberculosis, ikaba ifata cyane ibihaha ariko ishobora no gufata ibindi bice by’umubiri. Yandura mu mwuka igihe cyinshi.

IBIMENYETSO
- Inkorora irambye (>2–3 weeks), rimwe na rimwe amaraso
- Umuriro, kubira ibyuya nijoro, kunanuka, igahinda
- Kuribwa mu gituza, guhumeka nabi

RED FLAGS
- Hemoptysis nyinshi, gucika intege bikabije
- Suspected TB meningitis/abdominal TB (ibimenyetso bikomeye)
- Kwandura mu muryango (abana, abafite VIH) — ikurikirane ryihuse

IVURWA RY’IBANZE
- Kwihutira kwipimisha (sputum smear/Xpert MTB/RIF)
- Kwirinda gusangiza ibikoresho byegereye umunwa, kwambara agapfukamunwa

IVURWA RY’UMWUGA
- Gahunda y’imiti (HRZE) hakurikijwe UCG/NTLP protocols
- Gukurikirana adherence, kugenzura imbagara z’umubiri (LFTs) uko bikwiye
- Kwipimisha VIH, gutanga inama ku muryango

KWIYIRINDA
- Gukingira abana (BCG), gukurikiza ingamba zo kwirinda no gusukura aho abantu bateranira
- Gukurikirana abafatanye n’umurwayi

UMUJYANAMA W’UBUZIMA
- Sobanurira umurwayi akamaro ko kurangiza imiti, ingaruka zo kuyihagarika

INKOMOKO
UCG Rwanda 2023, Chapter 20.
