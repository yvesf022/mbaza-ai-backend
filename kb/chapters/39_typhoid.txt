IZINA RY’IGITABO: 39. TIFOYIDE

A. IBISOBANURO
Typhoid iterwa na Salmonella typhi/Paratyphi; yandurira mu biribwa/ibinyobwa byanduye.

B. IBIMENYETSO
- Umuriro uhora uri hejuru, kuribwa mu nda, isesemi, gucibwamo, rimwe na rimwe kwituma bigoye.
- Hepatosplenomegaly ishobora kuboneka.

C. ISUZUMA
- RDT/IgG/IgM ntizihagije zonyine; hemoculture/stooculture ni yo yizewe niba ibonetse. 
- CBC, LFTs niba bikenewe.

D. KUVURA
- Antibiotique ishingiye ku mabwiriza (ceftriaxone, azithromycin, ciprofloxacin bitewe na resistance y’akarere). 
- Rehydration, antipyretics.

E. USHAKA UMWUGA
- Shock, perforation ikekwa, kubura amazi gukabije, igisubizo kibi ku miti.

F. KWIRINDA
- Isuku y’amazi n’ibiribwa, gukaraba intoki, kubika neza.

G. IBYITONDERWA
- Kumenya resistance y’akarere ni ingenzi mu guhitamo imiti.
