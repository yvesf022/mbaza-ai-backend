IZINA RY’INDWARA
Appendicitis (Uturibwe tw’inyongera twafashwe)

IBISOBANURO
Ni uruhurirane rw’inkomyi/infekisiyo mu dukoresho twinyongera (appendix) dutera kuribwa mu nda ku ruhande rw’iburyo rw’epigastrium/umbilical bigatembera kuri RIF.

IBIMENYETSO
- Kuribwa mu nda bitangira ku nda hagati bikimukira kuri RIF
- Isesemi, kuruka, umuriro muke, anorexia
- Rebound tenderness

RED FLAGS
- Peritonitis: kuribwa gukabije, rigidity, hypotension
- Sepsis

IVURWA RY’UMWUGA
- Kwihutira kohereza mu buvuzi (surgical) — appendectomy
- Antibiotics mbere na nyuma y'ikora
- NPO, IV fluids, analgesics

KWIYIRINDA
- Nta bwo hari urukingo rudasanzwe; gusaba ubufasha hakiri kare

UMUJYANAMA W’UBUZIMA
- Sobanurira ibimenyetso byo kugaruka mu buvuzi byihuse

INKOMOKO
UCG Rwanda 2023, Chapter 24.
