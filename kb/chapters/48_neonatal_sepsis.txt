IZINA RY’INDWARA: Neonatal Sepsis (kwandura kwa ruhinja)

1) IBISOBANURO
Ukwandura gukomeye mu minsi 28 ya mbere; Early/late onset.

2) IBIMENYETSO
Kuramba/kuramba gusinzira, guhumeka nabi, gukonja/umuriro, kutanywa neza, cyanosis, convulsions.

3) ISUZUMA
• Vital signs, glucose yihuta.
• CBC, CRP niba bibashoboka, hemoculture, X-ray niba hari respiratory distress.

4) KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
• Ampicillin + gentamicin (dose zishingiye ku ibiro/imyaka).
• IV fluids, gushyushya umwana, O2 niba akenewe, kugenzura isukari.

5) IGIHE USHAKA UMWUGA
Guhumeka nabi, convulsions, shock, kutakira neza imiti.

6) KWIRINDA
Isuku mu kubyaza, gukaraba intoki, gukurikiza protocole ya cord care, gukomeza konka.

7) IBYITONDERWA
Kumenya ingaruka za gentamicin (oto-/nephrotoxicity), kugenzura dose.
