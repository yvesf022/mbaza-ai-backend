IZINA RY’INDWARA: Anemia (Kugabanuka k’amaraso)

IBISOBANURO:
Anemia ni uko umubiri ugira amaraso make (hemoglobine iri hasi), bigatuma umwuka mwiza (oxygen) utarinda kugera ku turemangingo uko bikwiye.

IBIMENYETSO BY’INGENZI:
- Umunaniro, gucika intege, umutima gukomanga vuba
- Kuzenguruka umutwe, guhuma amaso, kubura umwuka
- Uruhu rwera cyangwa rwirabura, inzara zoroshye zikavunika
- Ku bagore: imyenda y’ukwezi myinshi

IBYO GUKORA NONAHA:
- Kurya indyo ibonekamo ibyuma (fer): amahembe, ibishyimbo, isombe, imboga zifite icyatsi
- Kunywa fer na folic acid niba muganga yabitegetse
- Kwitwararika ku myanda y’imibu no ku bindi byatera kuva

RYARI KWIHUTIRA KWA MUGANGA:
- Guhumeka bigoye, kubura umwuka
- Umutima gukomanga cyane, kizungera kidasanzwe
- Kuvira cyane cyangwa anemia ukeka ko yatewe n’amaraso ari kuva

ICYITONDERWA:
Ibisubizo ni rusange. Kuboneza imirire, gusuzumwa kwa muganga no gukurikirana hemoglobine birakenewe.
