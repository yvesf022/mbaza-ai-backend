IZINA RY’IGITABO: 35. COPD

A. IBISOBANURO
COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease) ni indwara y’igihe kirekire yo mu bihaha itera kubura umwuka bitewe n’ihungabana ry’inzira z’ikirere (emphysema/bronchitis).

B. IBIMENYETSO BY’INGENZI
- Kuribwa n’umwuka, gukorora kenshi n’umusemburo, umunaniro wo guhumeka.
- Abashyitsi: abanywa itabi, abakorera mu mwanda/imyuka ihumanya.

C. ISUZUMA
- Anamnesis + auscultation, SpO2. 
- Spirometry niba ibonetse (FEV1/FVC<0.7). 
- R/O asthma, TB, CHF.

D. KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
- Itabi: guhagarika burundu. 
- Bronchodilators (SABA/SAMA), ICS+LABA ku barwara kenshi.
- Exacerbation: salbutamol neb, ipratropium, prednisolone 40 mg ×5–7, O2 (88–92%).

E. USHAKA UMWUGA
- SpO2<88% tudatanze O2, cyanosis, umuvuduko w’umutima mwinshi, kutihangana. 

F. KWIRINDA
- Guhagarika itabi, gupfuka akanda, inkingo (flu, pneumococcal), imyitozo.

G. IBYITONDERWA
- Kwitabira gukurikirana kwa muganga no gupimisha spirometry igihe biboneka.
