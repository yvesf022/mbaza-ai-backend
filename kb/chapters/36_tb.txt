IZINA RY’IGITABO: 36. IGASHA (TUBERCULOSIS)

A. IBISOBANURO
TB ni indwara iterwa na Mycobacterium tuberculosis, ikunze kwibasira ibihaha ariko ishobora no kwibasira ibindi bice.

B. IBIMENYETSO
- Gukorora igihe kirekire (>2 ibyumweru), uburiro, ibyuya nijoro, kunanuka, umuriro uri hasi. 
- Ku mwana: kunanuka, gukorora, ibimenyetso by’umurongo muto w’imbaraga.

C. ISUZUMA
- Sputum smear/Xpert MTB/RIF, X-ray y’ibihaha niba ibonetse, HIV test. 

D. KUVURA KU RWEGO RWA MBERE
- Guhuza umurongo wa gahunda y’igihugu (RHNT): fazi yo guhashya + yo gukurikirana (HRZE…).
- Kwubahiriza amahame ya DOTS, gutoza gufata imiti neza.

E. USHAKA UMWUGA
- Ibimenyetso bikomeye, HIV+, TB yoroheje yananiranye, akabariro (meningeal, m’ogutwi, igifu).

F. KWIRINDA
- Gukingira BCG, gukumira ikwirakwira (mask, umwuka uhagije), gusuzuma abo mubana.

G. IBYITONDERWA
- Imiti ya TB igira ingaruka; kugenzurwa n’urwego rubifitiye ububasha.
