IZINA: Kunanirwa k’umutima (Heart Failure)

IBISOBANURO:
— Uko umutima unanirwa gutera neza ngo usukure amaraso mu mubiri. Akenshi biterwa n’umutima wahuye n’imbogamizi (coronary disease, hypertension, cardiomyopathy).

IBIMENYETSO:
— Guhumeka nabi iyo uryamye cyangwa ukoresheje ingufu, kubyimba imirundi, kurakara vuba, umunaniro, kureka gukora imirimo isanzwe.

RED FLAGS:
— Guhumeka nabi cyane, cyanosis, umuvuduko w’amaraso uri hasi cyane, arythmia, cyangwa umusatsi w’amaraso mu makirenge (poor perfusion). Ahandi hoshi ni “pulmonary edema” (gupfundikanya n’inkorora y’amaraso).

IBIPIMO:
— EKG, X-ray y’ igituza, BNP/NT-proBNP (niba biboneka), echo y’umutima, laboratoire (renal function, electrolytes).

IVURWA:
— Diuretics (furosemide) ku kubyimba no guhumeka nabi, ACEI/ARB, beta-blocker (bisoprolol/ carvedilol), aldosterone antagonist (spironolactone), n’inyigisho z’imibereho (umunyu muke, kugenzura amazi).

ICYO KWITONDERA:
— Kwitwararika ku bunyamazi bwinshi, kugenzura ibiro buri munsi, kujya mu igenzura rihoraho.
