IZINA RY’INDWARA
Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

IBISOBANURO
COPD ni indwara y’igihe kirekire igira ingaruka ku myanya y’ubuhumekero, itera kugabanuka kudasubira k’umwuka winjira/usohoka, akenshi iterwa n’itabi cyangwa umwuka uhumanya igihe kirekire.

IBIMENYETSO
- Kuzirika mu gituza no guhumeka bigoranye buhoraho
- Inkorora y’igihe kirekire, akenshi ifite umusokoro
- Guhora unanirwa, kwiyongera kwa episodes (exacerbations)

RED FLAGS
- Hypoxia (SpO2 < 90%), cyanosis, gucika intege bikomeye
- Guhumeka guke cyane, confusion
- Suspected pneumonia/heart failure bifatanyije

IVURWA RY’IBANZE
- Kureka itabi byihutirwa
- Inhalers: bronchodilators (salbutamol), anticholinergics
- Imyitozo yo kongera imbaraga (pulmonary rehab), inama z’indyo

IVURWA RY’UMWUGA
- Nebulization, corticosteroids mu gihe cyiyongereye (exacerbation)
- Antibiotics niba hari ibimenyetso by’iyongera ry’umusokoro/infekisiyo
- Oxygene kuri hypoxia, kugenzura co-morbidities

KWIYIRINDA
- Guhagarika itabi, ikoreshwa ry’agapfukamunwa ahari umukungugu/imyuka
- Gupimwa buri gihe, gukurikiza imiti neza

UMUJYANAMA W’UBUZIMA
- Fasha ku nzira zo guhagarika itabi, supervisions y’imiti, gahunda yo kwitoza guhumeka neza
