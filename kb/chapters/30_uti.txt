IZINA: UTI (Urinary Tract Infection)

IBISOBANURO:
— Uburwayi buterwa n’udukoko mu nzira y’inkari (cystitis, pyelonephritis).

IBIMENYETSO:
— Gukomereka mu gihe cy’inkari, kwihuta kujya ku bwiherero, inkari zifite impumuro/kuzimira, umuriro (pyelonephritis: n’umugongo uremereye).

RED FLAGS:
— Umuriro mwinshi, kubabara umugongo hejuru (flank pain), inenge z’impyiko, gutwita.

IBIPIMO:
— Urinalysis (nitrite/leukocyte esterase), urine culture niba biboneka, test y’inkari ku bagore batwite.

IVURWA:
— Cystitis: nitrofurantoin, TMP-SMX (aho ikora), fosfomycin. Pyelonephritis: ciprofloxacin/amoxicillin-clavulanate bitewe n’imimerere; kunywa amazi menshi.

ICYO KWITONDERA:
— Gutwita bisaba imiti yihariye, kurangiza dose, gusuzuma niba igaruka kenshi.
